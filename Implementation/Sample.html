Our Journey

A decade of innovation, growth, and excellence in delivering technology solutions that transform businesses.

2014 - The Beginning

Started as Rubab J Consultancy offering comprehensive tech solutions, writing services, and HR consultancy. A small team with big dreams.

2016 - Expanding Horizons

Expanded our team and started specializing in web development and mobile applications. First major enterprise clients onboarded.

2018 - Technology Focus

Pivoted to focus primarily on software development and technology solutions. Introduced AI and machine learning services.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Helped businesses adapt to the digital-first world. Expanded remote services and cloud solutions. 200+ successful projects delivered.

2024 - TechCraft Solutions

Rebranded as TechCraft Solutions with specialized teams for each service area. Now serving 500+ clients globally with cutting-edge solutions.
